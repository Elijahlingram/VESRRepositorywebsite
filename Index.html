My Top Four Personal Values

Listening

1. talking rather than listening can cause more problems than just addressing the issue.

2. I have worked with people that have no issue interupting the authority as they are explaining what to do next on the job site.

3. This is not just aggrivating to listen to, but it can get in the way of just going ahead and getting the job done right the first time.

4. I developed this value by working with people that have no issue interupting the authority as the authority is explaining what to do next on the job site.

Hard Work

1. Hard work is always appreciated and often rewarded.

2. The harder you work the faster you get the job done.

3. If all people had the same idea and motivation as me with hard work, jobs would be done a lot faster.

4. When I was working on construction sites in the heat of the day. I always made sure to work hard, because if I got the job done fast I could got off early.

Efficiency through asking questions

1. Time is Money

2. Asking the right questions in the workplace leads to increased productivity.

3. When people ask questions to people with more years experience, it can help them understand more productive ways the get a job done fast and correctly.

4. I learned this value when I was installing the sound system at a football field. I was having trouble with part of the install, in other words my way was inefficient. So I asked my supervisor how he would get the job done. I took his word and got the job done way quicker.

understanding

1. Understanding is an important value because it helps build empathy and stronger relationships between people.

2. It allows us to see situations from others' perspectives, reducing conflict and promoting harmony.

3. Ultimately, it creates a foundation for cooperation, learning, and meaningful connection.

4. One week while working on a sound system in a school. I got sick with Covid. This slowed me down tremendously. My supervisor seeing my condition let me go home early and get some rest. Without the Supervisors understanding I don't know how I would have gotten through the rest of the day.
